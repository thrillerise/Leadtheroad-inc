Maldives Beaches

Experience the crystal clear waters and white sand beaches of the Maldives.

Explore The Maldives Beach

Begin your wonderful touring Experience in the Maldives, where pristine white-sand beaches meet crystal-clear turquoise waters.This tropical paradise offers luxurious resorts, vibrant marine life, and breathtaking sunsets,creating the perfect setting for relaxation and adventure by the Indian Ocean.

What to Experience:

  • Relax on the pristine beaches of the Maldives
  • Snorkel or dive among vibrant coral reefs
  • Indulge in a luxurious overwater spa experience
  • Enjoy a sunset cruise on a traditional dhoni boat

Package Includes:

  • 3 nights in a luxury overwater villa
  • Exclusive access to a private beach dinner
  • Guided snorkeling and island-hopping tour
  • Welcome feast featuring fresh seafood and tropical delicacies

Hong Kong Skyline

Explore the vibrant city of Hong Kong and take in its stunning skyline from Victoria Peak.

Experience The Hong Kong Skyline

The heart of Hong Kong, this dynamic metropolis is a global hub of culture, finance, and entertainment. Here, you’ll explore bustling street markets, savor world-class cuisine, and take in breathtaking views from Victoria Harbour, all while experiencing the vibrant energy of this iconic city.

What to Experience:

  • Explore the bustling markets of Mong Kok
  • Visit the iconic Tian Tan Buddha on Lantau Island
  • Take a scenic ride on the Peak Tram to Victoria Peak
  • Enjoy a sunset cruise in Victoria Harbour

Package Includes:

  • 5 nights in a luxury harbor-view hotel
  • Exclusive access to top Hong Kong attractions
  • Meet and dine with local cultural experts
  • Daily private transfers to iconic landmarks

Mauritius Landscapes

Visit the stunning coastal villages of Mauritius, where turquoise lagoons meet lush greenery

Discover The Mauritius Landscapes

Conclude your championship journey in Lucerne, a city of remarkable beauty where mountains meet lake waters. Known for its preserved medieval architecture and vibrant cultural scene, Lucerne offers the perfect setting for the closing celebrations of the championship.

What to Experience:

  • Attend a beachfront farewell celebration
  • Visit the stunning Seven Colored Earths of Chamarel
  • Take a catamaran cruise along the turquoise lagoons
  • Explore Black River Gorges National Park’s scenic trails

Package Includes:

  • 2 nights in a luxury beachfront resort
  • Exclusive access to a sunset beach celebration
  • Farewell gala dinner with island-inspired cuisine
  • Guided tour of Mauritius’ cultural and natural landmarks
