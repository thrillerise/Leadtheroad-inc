About LeadTheRoad

Vision and Growth

LeadTheRoad, Inc. is a leading tour operator in the US, headquartered in Chicago. Our vision is to provide unforgettable travel experiences while maintaining affordability and quality.


International Operations

We provide package tours to Thailand, Malaysia, Hong Kong, China, Maldives, Mauritius, New Zealand, and Singapore. Our strong partnerships with leading airlines and hotel chains ensure premium service at competitive prices.

In addition to package tours, we offer customized tours, incentive travel packages, online hotel reservations, business travels, and study tours.

Travel Tips

Our expert travel consultants provide comprehensive pre-trip guidance to ensure your journey is smooth and enjoyable. We offer personalized recommendations on packing essentials, local customs, currency exchange, and safety precautions tailored to your specific destination.

For adventure seekers, we provide specialized equipment lists and preparation advice. Our mobile app delivers real-time travel updates, weather forecasts, and emergency assistance during your trip.


Visa Processing

LeadTheRoad offers complete visa assistance services for all destinations in our portfolio. Our dedicated visa team handles document preparation, application submission, and follow-up with embassies and consulates to ensure timely processing.

We maintain strong relationships with diplomatic missions worldwide, allowing us to provide accurate information on changing visa requirements and expedite applications when necessary. Our visa success rate exceeds 98% for all destination countries.

Frequently Asked Questions

What destinations do you offer travel packages to?

LeadTheRoad offers travel packages to Thailand, Malaysia, Hong Kong, China, Maldives, Mauritius, New Zealand, Singapore, and special tours for the World Skiing Championship. Our team is continuously expanding our portfolio to include new exciting destinations.

How do I book a tour with LeadTheRoad?

Booking a tour is simple! Just fill out our booking form in the "Book Your Trip" section of our website. Select your desired destination, travel dates, and number of travelers. Once submitted, our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm your booking and provide further details.

Do you provide visa assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive visa assistance for all destinations in our portfolio. Our dedicated visa team handles document preparation, application submission, and follow-up with embassies and consulates. We maintain a 98% success rate for visa applications across all destination countries.

Can I customize my travel package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized travel experiences. When booking, simply mention your specific requirements in the "Special Requirements" section. Our travel consultants will work with you to design a personalized itinerary that matches your interests, preferences, and budget.

What is included in your standard travel packages?

Our standard packages include accommodation, airport transfers, guided tours, and some meals (specified in the itinerary). International flights are typically not included but can be arranged upon request. Travel insurance, visa fees, and personal expenses are also not included in the base package.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and selected digital payment platforms. For your security, all online payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy offers a full refund (minus administrative fees) for cancellations made at least 45 days before departure. Cancellations between 44-30 days receive a 50% refund, while those made within 29 days of departure are non-refundable. However, we do offer flexible booking options and travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer attractive group discounts for parties of 6 or more travelers booking the same package. The discount percentage increases with group size. For corporate groups or special events, please contact our team directly for customized packages and pricing.
