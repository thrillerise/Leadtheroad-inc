World Skiing Championship

Experience the thrill of world-class skiing in Switzerland's most breathtaking locations. Join us for an unforgettable journey through Zurich, Davos, and Lucerne.

Book Championship Package

Championship Overview

The Ultimate Skiing Experience

LeadTheRoad is proud to be the official tour operator for the 2025 World Skiing Championship. This prestigious event brings together the world's top skiers across multiple stunning locations in Switzerland.

Our exclusive package includes VIP access to all championship events, luxury accommodations, and guided tours of Switzerland's most iconic cities. Whether you're a skiing enthusiast or simply love to experience world-class sporting events in beautiful settings, this package offers an unforgettable experience.

Event Schedule

Duration: 10 days

Dates: April 5-15, 2025

Main Events:

  • Opening Ceremony in Zurich (April 5)
  • Men's Alpine Skiing in Davos (April 7-9)
  • Women's Alpine Skiing in Davos (April 10-12)
  • Championship Finals in Davos (April 14)
  • Closing Ceremony in Lucerne (April 15)

Featured Locations

Zurich

Switzerland's largest city, the gateway to your skiing adventure

Explore Zurich

Begin your championship journey in Zurich, a city that perfectly blends urban sophistication with natural beauty. As the financial center of Switzerland, Zurich offers world-class shopping, dining, and cultural experiences, all set against the stunning backdrop of Lake Zurich and the Alps.

What to Experience:

  • Attend the Championship Opening Ceremony
  • Tour the historic Old Town (Altstadt)
  • Visit the renowned Kunsthaus art museum
  • Enjoy a cruise on Lake Zurich

Package Includes:

  • 3 nights in a luxury hotel
  • VIP access to Opening Ceremony
  • Guided city tour
  • Welcome dinner at a traditional Swiss restaurant

Davos

Tour the championship venue and experience world-class slopes

Experience Davos

The heart of the World Skiing Championship, Davos is Europe's highest city and one of Switzerland's most prestigious ski resorts. Here you'll witness elite athletes competing on some of the most challenging slopes in the world while enjoying the exclusive amenities of this world-famous alpine destination.

What to Experience:

  • Attend the Men's and Women's Championship events
  • Visit the Skiing Museum
  • Take a scenic ride on the Parsenn funicular
  • Enjoy apres-ski activities with international guests

Package Includes:

  • 5 nights in a premium mountain resort
  • All-access passes to championship events
  • Meet and greet with skiing champions
  • Daily shuttle to competition venues

Lucerne

Visit the picturesque lakeside city near the championship location

Discover Lucerne

Conclude your championship journey in Lucerne, a city of remarkable beauty where mountains meet lake waters. Known for its preserved medieval architecture and vibrant cultural scene, Lucerne offers the perfect setting for the closing celebrations of the championship.

What to Experience:

  • Attend the Closing Ceremony celebration
  • Visit the iconic Chapel Bridge and Water Tower
  • Take a boat cruise on Lake Lucerne
  • Explore Mount Pilatus via the world's steepest cogwheel railway

Package Includes:

  • 2 nights in a lakeside hotel
  • VIP seating at the Closing Ceremony
  • Farewell gala dinner
  • Guided tour of Lucerne's historic sites
